ABOUT

Voting is the process of choosing officials or policies by casting a ballot. Republics and representative democracies are governments where the population chooses representatives by voting. The procedure for identifying winners varies depending on country and political system. Political scientists call these procedures electoral systems, while mathematicians and economists call them social choice rules.
